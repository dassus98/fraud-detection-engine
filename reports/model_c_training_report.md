## Architecture

- 3-layer GraphSAGE (`SAGEConv`) + Linear head; per spec.
- Mean aggregator; bipartite undirected graph (txn ↔ entity).
- Neighbor sampling per layer via `NeighborLoader`.
- Focal loss (α=0.25, γ=2.0) imported from `neural_model.FocalLoss`.
- Float32 throughout the numeric pipeline.
